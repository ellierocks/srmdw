## Our Commitment to Quality

speedrun.md is a community-driven wiki built on trust and accuracy. All content
is written and reviewed by humans. We do not use AI-generated content.

## Content Policy

### Human-Written Content Only

All wiki pages must be written by humans. This includes:

- Original explanations and guides written from personal knowledge
- Information transcribed from reliable sources with proper attribution
- Community-verified speedrun strategies and routing

### Prohibited Content

The following is not allowed:

- **AI-generated content:** No ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or other AI-written
  pages
- **Copied content:** Do not copy from other wikis, strategy guides, or sources
  without proper attribution
- **Speculation presented as fact:** Route descriptions must be verified
- **Personal opinions:** Keep content objective and factual
- **Unverified glitches:** Document only glitches that have been confirmed by
  the community

### Attribution

When referencing information from external sources:

- Include a citation or link to the original source
- Transcribed content should note "transcribed from [source]"
- Video strategies should link to the original video

### Content Format

- Write in clear, concise English
- Use consistent terminology throughout a vault
- Follow the [Markdown Reference](/docs/markdown) for formatting guidelines
- Frontmatter is required for all pages

### Frontmatter Requirements

Every page requires frontmatter:

```markdown
---
title: "Page Title"
description: "Optional description for SEO and previews."
tags: ["optional", "tags"]
---
```

### Editing Guidelines

- Keep edits focused and minimal
- Large rewrites should be discussed in an issue first
- When in doubt, add a note or comment rather than removing content
- Respect existing writing styles within each vault

## Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

1. Request for revision before merging
2. Rejection of the contribution
3. Removal of existing AI-generated content

## Questions?

If you are unsure whether content meets these guidelines, please open an issue
before submitting a pull request.
